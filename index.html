Background

Beginning
My passion for computer started when I was 10, playing on my uncle's computer and learning how to install programs and games using the Floppy 3.5. Windows 95 was my first OS and I loved it. At the age of 14 I got my first computer and I've started on my own to expirence how to fix problems, crash and changing the components inside it.
School
In High School I have decided to study Computer Science. Here I've learn Java as programming language and how IT infostructures works. At this time I was not sure that was what I want to do in my life, I was more interested into repairing and modding computers.
Work
After graduating, I was hired as a computer technician and my job duties were to fix old computers and assemble new ones. In the same corporate group I switched to distribution and entertainment, where I worked with machines that interfaced with assembled computers. At the end of my career in that company I was transferred to the support branch as an IT-Supporter for external customers and internally helping with IT-infrastructure configurations. It was here that I met the frontend developer of the company who showed me his website and what he was building for the company and from that moment on that my journey began.
