Case & Develop

This was a group project where we were challanged to create, brand and sell a product from scratch. We had to desing it, taking picture, collecting sponsors, advertising it on social media platform and create a website that appeal our customers target.

We started from define our clients target to shaping around our idea a new and innovative way to approch and advertise it. We used the Discord layout to create our website that intagrate the chat from it to be always update with the last deals that we have to offer to our users.

The website is entire build with HTML, CSS and Javascript. It is fully responsive, but it's miss the final touch to be ready to get implemented on web.

Development time

The project was accomplished in a period of 3/4 weeks.
