Case & Develop

The theme of this project is Music Festival in Denmark. I had to develop around it a website that is was focusing to advertise a product that is part of a music festival.

Logo and design are both my ideas, with the focus of creating something smart that is was thinking about sustainability and recycling.

The website is entire build with HTML, SCSS and a bit of Javascript. It is fully responsive and I developed it using the Mobile First approach.

Development time

The project was accomplished in a period of 2/3 weeks.
